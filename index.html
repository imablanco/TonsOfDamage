TonsOfDamage

TonsOfDamage is your League of Legends Android app for stay tuned and know everything about League of Legends

It uses Teemo library for comunicate with Riot API.

With TonsOfDamage, you cand have all the information about LoL at your fingertips. Read everything about your favorite champions, items, summoners, etc.

Customize the app to see your games, stats, champion mastery and so much more. You can even mark your favorite champions and summoners to have them always at your sight.

Try now TonsOfDamage and start enjoying.
